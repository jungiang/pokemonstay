Welcome trainer to the wonderful world of pokemon

In this game, you can capture pokemon as you match two of a kind. Simply click on the card backs and reveal what pokemon awaits you.

If you fail in getting a pair, the pokemon will flee. Fear not, they will still stay in the area so you can simply try again.

If you capture every pokémon in a round, it will move on to the next day and re-populate the fields. If you are having a tough time, you can also reset the board by clicking on the JigglyPuff button and have her sing you to sleep.

Once captured, the pokemon will remain in your collection even if you close the game! Just click on the "Pokémon Collection" to see everything you have captured so far.

I will be keeping track of how many days you have spent on your journey, how many pokemon total you have caught, and even your accuracy in each round. You can find all those details on the main page!

However, if you would like to reset your entire journey including everything you have caught, you can find the "Reset Journey" button on the collection page.

To start you off, I'll leave this pikachu in your care!

If you need any more assistance, then you may come back to me with the "Game Menu" button on the main page.

There are 151 discovered pokemon in our world, so enjoy your time exploring and making new friends.
